Enable phone sign-in and live Passwordless
Enable phone sign-in and live Passwordless
03 March | CIO Organization

Enable phone sign-in via the Microsoft Authenticator app to meet Passwordless requirements. Here’s how:  

  1. Open the app on your phone.
  2. Click your Accenture email – or add your Accenture email if not shown - and select ‘Enable phone sign in’.
  3. Ensure Microsoft Authenticator is shown as the default method on My Sign-Ins page. 

Now your account will be prioritized for upgrade to full Passwordless!
